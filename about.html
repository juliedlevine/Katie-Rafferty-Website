ABOUT KATIE

Text about Katie. Background, education etc.

New Orleans artist Katie Rafferty is both a printmaker and painter who often combines textiles, fabric patterns, newspaper clippings, and her own prints into her collaged oil paintings. If her work is not directly inspired by literature or local flora, then Rafferty often works with imagery that correlates to the New Orleans urban experience.
